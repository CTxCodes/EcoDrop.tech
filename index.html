The Growing Pollution Epidemic

18 billion pounds of plastic trash winds up in our oceans each year. That is actually enough trash to cover every foot of the coastline around the world with five full trash bags of plastic compounding every year.

In the United States, we throw away 2.5 million plastic bottles every hour - about 42,000 per minute or about 695 per second.

The EcoDrop Solution

EcoDrop uses a reverse vending machine to simplify and incentivize recycling for college students. Our Process is simple. Recycle, Redeem, and Reduce.

Recycle

Recycle your plastic bottles and aluminum cans with our EcoDrop machines for points.

Redeem

Redeem your points for discounts on products and services within your community.

Reduce

Reduce your carbon footprint and increase your recycling habits!
