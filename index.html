WHAT WE DO?

Recycle

We take your plastic and aluminum bottles and recycle them into their respective containers with our Reverse Vending Machine!

Reward

Once the user recycles with our machine they will recieve points and experience. As the user recieves points and levels up they will recieve better rewards!

Market

Your business and brand will be able to grow by the items that you would like to sponsor as rewards on our application!

WHY CHOOSE US?

Why we're different

  • We are gamifying the recycling process by creating an interactive community through our application and reverse vending machine!
  • We bring the customers to you by offering your products as rewards for recycling bottles!
  • With our single insertion machine recycling your bottles will be quick and easy!
  • We offer a unique and sleek design for a reverse vending machine so people will be able to locate and recognize us from a distance!
  • We care about and listen to our users to create the beset possible experience!
  • All of our recycled material will be used to better the earth and you!
